About Me

Cloud Architect | DevOps Engineer | Problem Solver with a Systems Mindset

My journey into cloud engineering began with a single question: How can we build systems that don’t just work, but adapt, scale, and defend themselves in the face of chaos?

Since then, I’ve spent the last few years helping teams—startups, nonprofits, and growing tech companies—answer that question by designing resilient infrastructures across AWS, Google Cloud, and hybrid environments. I’ve led cloud migrations, built multi-region architectures, and automated CI/CD pipelines for mission-critical applications—all while ensuring security, compliance, and cost control were never afterthoughts.

The Tools I Use to Solve Real Problems

  • Infrastructure as Code: Terraform, CloudFormation, Ansible
  • CI/CD Automation: GitHub Actions, Jenkins, AWS CodePipeline
  • Observability: Prometheus, Grafana, CloudWatch
  • Security: IAM, MFA, AWS WAF, encryption protocols

A Few Wins I'm Proud Of

  • Reduced cloud spend by 30% for a nonprofit through intelligent rightsizing and automation.
  • Enabled zero-downtime releases for a fintech platform by restructuring CI/CD pipelines.
  • Cut incident detection time in half by integrating full-stack observability and alerting systems.
  • Migrated legacy systems to the cloud while maintaining uptime and regulatory compliance.

What Drives Me

I believe that infrastructure should be invisible until it’s needed. It should heal itself, scale itself, and secure itself — all while empowering developers to move fast without breaking things. That’s the ethos I bring to every system I design and every team I support.
